Competition Structure

Five stages to the championship

01
School Round
All registered students sit a written exam at their own school.
02
Second Round
Top performers from Round 1 compete again in a harder exam.
03
Third Round
The best students from each school advance. A rigorous exam narrows the field towards the semi-final.
04
Semi-Final
Top qualifiers from Round 3 compete in a centralised semi-final. The top 20–80 students advance to the Grand Finale.
05
Grand Finale
20–80 elite finalists compete live at Adwa Museum, Addis Ababa. Written exam. Live audience. Prize ceremony.

FAQ

Common questions

Who can register?
Any Grade 11 or Grade 12 student enrolled in a government or private secondary school in Addis Ababa.
How does the 500 ETB fee work?
The 500 ETB fee covers participation certificate, competition materials, and contributes to the prize pool.
How do I apply for the low-income waiver?
Check the low-income waiver box when registering. Your teacher will review and select qualifying students.
What subjects are tested?
All core subjects for your stream. Natural Science and Social Science subjects are included.
Where are the first four rounds held?
Rounds 1, 2, and 3 are held at your own school. Round 4 (Semi-Final) is at a centralised venue. Only the top 20–80 students travel to Adwa Museum for the Grand Finale (Round 5).
Is this competition officially approved?
Yes. This is organized in partnership with the Addis Ababa City Education Office.
